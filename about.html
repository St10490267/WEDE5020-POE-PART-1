About the Initiative

Our Mission

To reduce drowning incidents in Mabopane by providing accessible aquatic education to every child, regardless of their financial background.

Our Vision

A community where water safety is a fundamental skill and the youth are empowered through fitness and discipline.

Who We Serve

Our primary focus is on learners from local primary and secondary schools in the Mabopane and Winterveldt areas.
